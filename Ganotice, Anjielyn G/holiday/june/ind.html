Independence Day

Independence Day is a national holiday that commemorates the historic moment when a country gained its freedom from colonial rule or foreign domination. It is a day of celebration, patriotism, and remembrance of those who fought for sovereignty.

What is Independence Day?

Independence Day marks the official recognition of a nation's autonomy. It is celebrated with parades, flag-raising ceremonies, fireworks, and cultural events. In many countries, this day serves as a reminder of the sacrifices made by national heroes who fought for freedom.

People commemorate this day by honoring the national flag, singing the national anthem, and participating in various patriotic activities. It is a time for unity, pride, and reflection on the country's progress.
