José Rizal’s Birthday

José Rizal, the national hero of the Philippines, was born on **June 19, 1861** in Calamba, Laguna. He was a writer, patriot, and revolutionary who played a key role in inspiring the Philippine revolution against Spanish colonial rule.

Who is José Rizal?

José Rizal was a polymath—fluent in multiple languages, a skilled doctor, and a passionate reformist. His literary works, **"Noli Me Tangere"** and **"El Filibusterismo,"** exposed the injustices of Spanish rule and ignited the flames of nationalism among Filipinos.

Rizal's legacy continues to inspire Filipinos to fight for freedom, education, and justice. His birthday is commemorated as a time to reflect on his contributions and the importance of national identity.
