National Heroes Day

National Heroes Day is a public holiday in the Philippines observed every **last Monday of August** to honor the courage and sacrifices of the country’s heroes who fought for freedom and independence.

Why is National Heroes Day Important?

This day is dedicated to remembering the bravery of both well-known and unnamed heroes who played significant roles in the country’s history, from **Lapu-Lapu, José Rizal, Andres Bonifacio, and Emilio Aguinaldo**, to the countless Filipinos who stood against oppression.

Filipinos commemorate this day with **ceremonies, wreath-laying events, and educational discussions** to instill patriotism and respect for those who fought for the nation’s freedom.
