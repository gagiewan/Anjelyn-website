Merry Christmas!

**Merry Christmas** is celebrated worldwide on **December 25th** to commemorate the birth of **Jesus Christ**. It is a day of joy, love, and giving, bringing families, friends, and communities together to celebrate the spirit of generosity and goodwill.

What is Christmas?

Christmas is both a **religious holiday** celebrating the birth of Jesus Christ and a **cultural event** marked by various traditions, such as **gift-giving, feasts, and decorations**. People around the world celebrate with different customs, but the central themes of love, kindness, and family remain universal.

In the Philippines, Christmas is celebrated with great enthusiasm, starting as early as September. Families decorate their homes with Christmas lights and lanterns, and the Christmas season culminates with **Noche Buena** on Christmas Eve, followed by **Simbang Gabi** services.
